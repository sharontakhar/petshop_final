Before we knew it my small meals for Molly became a little weekend venture, being sold at the Sunday market. From there we grew to selling local shops across the area. in 2018 we reached milestone and started selling our range in Waitrose! All our receipes are organic and made from the freshest product grown nearby and Love!! We hope your pets enjoy them as much as our do.
